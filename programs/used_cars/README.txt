A collection of graphs and tables related to the used car market in Poland and Switzerland. This was created based on data from the largest used car selling websites in both countries.

Graphs and tables can be found in the folders analiza/wykresy_CH and analiza/wykresy_PL.
The script to generate them is located in the main folder.
The raw data, on which the analysis was based, can be found in /analiza/czysteDaneSamochodowWybraneModeleXX.csv, where XX stands for either CH or PL.

The data was collected in August 2024
