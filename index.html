Research

I am a member of the Applied Algebraic Geometry Group led by Prof. Dr. Jan Draisma . Our research is focused on tensors, their geometry and properties, with applications to complexity theory.
  • tensor ranks (rank, border rank, asymptotic rank, etc.)
  • Veronese, Segre and Segre-Veronese varieties
  • Secant varieties
  • Polynomial functors
